Why magico?

Our spellbinding methodology

💨

It's all smoke and mirrors

Our methodology is simple: we watch what you do, write it down on a clipboard, and present it back to you in a nicer font. The real magic? Even we're not entirely sure how it works. But it does. Every time.

🔮

We say 'it depends' — but professionally

Every problem gets a 2×2 matrix. Every solution gets a Greek letter. We turn good ideas into great slide decks, and great slide decks into real results. It's not rocket science. It's better — it's magic.

🎩

Every trick needs a lovely assistant

We don't just leave — we leave behind a legacy of shiny new acronyms, frameworks your team will actually use, and a newfound enthusiasm for the phrase 'value stream'. Collaboration is at the core of everything we do. You're welcome.
