Our methodology

How we actually work

Our three-phase approach has been refined over dozens of engagements. We have given each phase a name to make it sound more deliberate than it is.

📋

Phase 1: We watch you do things and write them down

Our consultants will stand in your factory with a clipboard for several weeks. They will then present your own processes back to you in a nicer font. The real skill is maintaining a facial expression that suggests this required a postgraduate degree. Which, to be fair, it did.

📈

Phase 2: We put it in a graph and say 'look at that'

Every problem gets a 2×2 matrix. Every solution gets a Greek letter. We turn your gut feelings into 80-page slide decks with executive summaries that say exactly what you already knew, but with better axis labels and a colour palette that tested well in focus groups.

🙋

Phase 3: We leave and you take the credit

After several months we go home and you present our findings as your own at the next board meeting. This is the correct and expected outcome. We get paid, you get promoted, the factory runs 30% better. Everybody wins. Nobody mentions us again until the next problem.

We are immensely grateful to our brand partners at yaya for helping us realise this bold new vision. When we said 'we want to lean into the hindsight angle', they maintained steady eye contact, nodded slowly, and charged us accordingly.
