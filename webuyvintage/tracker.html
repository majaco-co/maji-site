Weeks 6–9

Model Validation & Tuning

Take PoC prototypes to production quality: backtesting, cross-validation, confidence intervals, and edge-case handling.

Backtest all models against held-out data
Hold out the most recent 6 months of data. Train models on history, predict the held-out period, measure accuracy. Proves predictions are reliable, not just plausible.
To Do Wk 6–7
Cross-validation across regions and time periods
Ensure models generalise and aren't overfitting to specific regions or seasonal patterns. K-fold validation by region.
To Do Wk 7
Add confidence intervals to all model outputs
Every recommendation should carry a confidence score so the ops team knows how much to trust each prediction. High-data areas get tight intervals; sparse areas get wide ones.
To Do Wk 7–8
Edge-case handling
New venues with no history, new dealers with <5 events, areas with sparse data, seasonal anomalies (e.g. COVID residuals). Models must degrade gracefully, not produce misleading outputs.
To Do Wk 8

To Do Wk 8–9
