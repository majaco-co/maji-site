Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2025

majaco Limited ("majaco", "we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect the information you provide when using the maji platform at majiai.co and related services.

Who We Are

majaco Limited is a registered company in England and Wales. Our registered office is at 6th Floor, 9 Appold Street, London, EC2A 2AP. We are the data controller for personal information collected through this website.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of personal information:

  • Contact information: name, email address, phone number, company name
  • Enquiry information: the content of messages you send us through our contact form
  • Technical information: IP address, browser type, pages visited, time spent on pages (via analytics tools)
  • Cookie data: see our Cookie section below

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to your enquiries and provide the services you've requested
  • Send you information about our services where you have requested it
  • Improve our website and services based on usage analytics
  • Comply with our legal obligations

Legal Basis for Processing

We process your personal data under the following lawful bases:

  • Consent: where you have ticked the consent box on our contact form
  • Legitimate interests: for improving our services and website performance
  • Legal obligation: where we are required to process data by law

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information with:

  • Service providers who assist in our operations (e.g., email service providers, analytics), bound by confidentiality agreements
  • Professional advisers (legal, accounting) where necessary
  • Regulatory authorities where required by law

Data Retention

We retain personal data for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes for which it was collected, and as required by applicable law. Contact enquiry data is typically retained for 3 years unless you ask us to delete it sooner.

Your Rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Object to processing or restrict processing
  • Data portability
  • Withdraw consent at any time (where processing is based on consent)

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at the address below.

Cookies

We use cookies to improve your experience on our website. We use:

  • Essential cookies: required for the website to function correctly
  • Analytics cookies: help us understand how visitors use the site (e.g., Google Analytics)
  • Marketing cookies: only set with your explicit consent

You can manage your cookie preferences at any time.

Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorised access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. Our website is served over HTTPS.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by posting the new policy on this page with an updated date.
